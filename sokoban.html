Sokoban

Welcome!

This version of Sokoban is programmed by Woon Him WONG from IDM in 2018.
The goal of the game is to move all box on the screen to their designated checkpoints.

Instructions

Use the arrow keys on the keyboard to move the player around the grid map.
The next level will be loaded when all box is placed on checkpoints.

If you get stuck, click the Reset Level button on the right to restart the level.
